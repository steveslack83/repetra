Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 8, 2026

Overview

Repetra is designed to help you build better habits through daily tracking and motivation. We respect your privacy and believe that your personal data should remain yours.

Data Collection

Repetra does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All habit tracking and progress data is stored locally on your device and never shared with us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Repetra does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Your Control

You have full control over your data. You can reset your progress or delete the app at any time without leaving any trace behind.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever introduce features that require data handling, we will update this policy and notify users clearly and transparently.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, please reach out via our GitHub repository or support channel.
